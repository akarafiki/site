AFRICA MALE SUPERMODEL DISCOVERY

Africa Male Supermodel Discovery is a TV reality show designed to discover Young/ Talented Male Models in Africa and give them an International platform to project their skills. The TV show will air for a full season on national and International channels.

THE HUNT

Frank Osodi and his model scout team will go on an adventure to 16 African countries in search of young male talents after which 16 of them will be shortlisted for the TV Reality show that will be filmed in a geographic African location and broadcast to the World on Satellite and Online Television.

THE STYLE

The Male Models will be camped, drilled, trained and mentored by Top Photographers, designers and Model scouts from all over the world. Weekly Elimination will take place after different tasks; at the end only one man will win as the number one Male Supermodel in Africa. This Process is designed to project maximum entertainment while discovering the best talents in Africa.
Africa is said to be the next “it” continent and in Africa, this industry that legally employs the youths all over the world must not be left out.
The Broadcast Reach for Africa’s Male Supermodel Discovery is totally endless.

PRODUCTION

The Reality show will be Filmed by Tiger Republic Films. A Television and Film Company Based in Europe, America and Africa.

So Far Tiger Republic has filmed ground breaking and Award winning TV Commercial, Live shows, Short Films, and Documentaries. The Vision for this project is to produce the best TV Reality show ever to come out of Africa.

Creator/Producer

This project is being put together by BUNOR CREAZIONI, owned by Nigerian fashion mogul, Frank Osodi Richard. Frank a former professional and West African top model has won for himself several awards over the years as a fashion designer, most notable among them are International designer of the year 1991-1992, fashion person of the year 2003. Frank is also the brain behind the highly successful annual international fashion show BLACK, BOLD AND JAZZY, and respectfully the organizer of the West African fashion week. It is important to state at this point that he dressed the very first black African to win the miss world crown, AGBANI DAREGO.


With the roots of his talent stemming from fashion designing and fashion consultancy, model management, media events and make up artistry. He was once the vice president of the Fashion Designers Association of Nigeria, FADAN. Frank has over the years showcased his creations at so many fashion events. His is a regular feature at the South Africa fashion week, Vukani fashion awards, Mozambique fashion week, Africa fashion week London, PAHBA awards, Miss Africa U.S.A; Atlanta and Barbados and a host of other fashion fairs all over the world.

He is currently the president of the model managers association of Nigeria (PROMMAN). He believes that Africa’s Male Supermodel Discovery is the next big thing to come from Africa to the World.

PARTICIPANT APPLICATION

Please read the preceding Eligibility Requirements before completing this application. The Eligibility Requirements contain important instructions regarding the videotape, photographs and other items, which must accompany any submitted participant application, as well as submission guidelines for your application package. And remember, above all else, you must BE HONEST! Any answer may be verified for accuracy. Don't answer a question hoping to give us the answer we "want" to hear.

PLEASE NOTE: You must be AT OR ABOVE five feet and ten inches [5’10”] in height to be eligible although Producers reserve the right to make case-by-case exceptions. However, the Producers are looking for a variety of models.

Participants on the show will be required to travel internationally and therefore are required to have a valid international passport.

If you are currently represented by an agent or manager and are selected to be interviewed, you must furnish sufficient evidence to the Producers that you are able to terminate such representation in the event that you are selected to continue on in the casting for the show. Alternatively, you may be permitted to keep your representation as long as it does not interfere in any way with your ability to accept all prizes or otherwise perform the obligations of being selected to participate and actually participate in the show.

You may be required to terminate any representation even if it does not conflict with the terms of your participation.
